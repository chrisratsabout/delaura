Releases

Ruptura

“Ruptura” is DEL AURA’s debut album filled with dense layers of ‘80s-vibe synthesizers and spacey soundscapes. The title track was written as far back as 2014 and the rest of the music took shape in 2018 - 2019. Derek wrote the lyrics and recorded the vocals in June - August 2020.

Undefined

Technical guitar riffs, odd-time signatures, abrupt transitions, and the exploration of other musical genres are all found on their second effort “Undefined”. The title track’s earliest demo dates back to 2010 and the other songs were written when the two met in the summer of 2014. Emily provided additional vocals on “Niebla”, “Myriad”, and “Eight Minute Rays”, and Carlos played the sax on “Hemispheres, Pt. 1”.

Moratorium

“Moratorium” features Derek as the main songwriter and performer on this release. These songs were worked on during the initial collaboration in 2014 and also existed as rough demos. Luckily, the original guitar takes from 2014 were still usable, leaving the two without much left to redo. In January 2021, Derek recorded his vocals again while Chris played bass and learned how to play the recorder for “What Lies Beyond Truth”.

Concordia

“Concordia” is a reimagined version of the debut album “Ruptura”. Despite the change in genre, each song retains the main musical motifs from the original version. Following an unorthodox approach, the pre-existing vocal melodies shaped the music that was composed afterward.
